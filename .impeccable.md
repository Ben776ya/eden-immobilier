## Design Context

### Users
High-net-worth individuals buying luxury properties in Morocco (Casablanca and surrounding areas). They expect a premium digital experience that reflects the exclusivity of the properties and the caliber of service. They browse on both desktop and mobile, often on high-end devices with retina displays.

### Brand Personality
**Warm, Elegant, Personal** — approachable luxury with high-touch service. The feeling of being welcomed into an exclusive but human experience. Not cold or institutional — more like a trusted advisor who happens to have impeccable taste.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Dark luxury with gold accents — editorial, refined, quiet confidence
- **Theme**: Dark mode exclusively (eden-bg #0F0D0D base)
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (serif display) + DM Sans (sans body) — warm but prestigious
- **Color palette**: Near-black, warm gold (#C9A84C), cream (#F5F0E8), warm muted tones
- **Anti-references**: NOT generic real estate portals (SeLoger, Zillow) — no utilitarian grids. NOT flashy tech startups — no neon, glassmorphism, or SaaS aesthetics. This is a luxury brand, not a listing platform.
- **References**: Think quiet luxury maisons — understated, confident, every detail intentional

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth through restraint** — Let generous whitespace and warm tones create intimacy, not coldness
2. **Every detail is intentional** — No decorative clutter; each element earns its place
3. **Elevated simplicity** — Simple layouts executed with impeccable craft (spacing, typography, transitions)
4. **Service-first hierarchy** — The properties and the human connection are front and center, not the UI
5. **Consistent luxury signals** — Gold accents, wide letter-spacing labels, serif headlines, and smooth transitions used systematically
